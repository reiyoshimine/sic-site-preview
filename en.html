Services

What We Offer

Regular home visits by a physician,
fully covered by Japanese health insurance

Who it's for

  • Anyone who finds it difficult to visit a clinic
  • Patients continuing recovery at home after hospital discharge
  • Residents of nursing homes and care facilities
  • Palliative and end-of-life care at home
  • International residents who prefer care in English

What we do

  • Scheduled medical visits and health management
  • Prescriptions, IV therapy, blood tests
  • Wound and pressure-sore care
  • Dementia and mental-health care (with specialist advisors)
  • Referrals and coordination with hospitals
  • Medical certificates and paperwork support

How It Works

Getting Started

Costs are covered by Japanese health insurance (10–30% co-pay).
Subsidy programs may reduce your costs — just ask us.

1

Call us

Patients, family members, care managers, and hospital social workers are all welcome to get in touch. English is fine.

2

Meet & plan

We discuss your condition and wishes, explain services and costs, and create a personalized care plan.

3

Visits begin

Regular visits start — typically twice a month. If your condition changes, call us anytime.

Get in Touch

Questions about home-visit care? Feel free to call us — in English or Japanese.
